Home Assistant Blinds

5 September, 2018

20:37

 

Not Obvious

Getting the blinds working correctly in Home-Assistant was not that obvious. Also we found little information on the web.

The major problem is that the buttons (see picture below) are detected as normal On/Off Switches, while they are momentary push buttons, forming together a  4-state switch.

 

Hardware

For the blinds we now have the following controls:

Two relais, each with a single ompoolschakelaar, die geheel vrij te bedraden zijn.

In this way you can control blinds up and down, without the chance that both lines are active.

The RF-chips are the EV1527 QIA-chips.

The RF control is done by RFLink-ESP.

 

 

Home Assistant Connection

Having trouble implementing the RFLink connection, we decided to connect through MQTT-broker which is much easier (RFLink-ESP was modified to connet also through the MQTT-broker).
